Uttar Pradesh: India's Largest Market

Uttar Pradesh is India's most populous state with over 23 crore people (16% of India's population) and contributes 8.4% to India's GDP. With annual revenue exceeding ₹21.5 lakh crore, the state offers the largest domestic market in India and accounts for 18% of India's agricultural output.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • Agricultural Output: 18% of India's total
