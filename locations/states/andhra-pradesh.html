Andhra Pradesh: Cyberabad & Innovation Hub

Andhra Pradesh is India's technology and pharmaceutical powerhouse, contributing over 4.2% to India's GDP with annual revenue exceeding ₹11.8 lakh crore. Hyderabad, known as "Cyberabad," hosts 20% of India's pharmaceutical companies and accounts for 15% of India's IT exports, making it a global technology and life sciences destination.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • GDP: ₹11.84 lakh crore (2023-24)
  • IT Exports: 15% of India's total
  • Pharmaceutical Production: 20% of India's pharma
  • Biotechnology: 30% of India's biotech companies
  • Aerospace: Major defense and aerospace hub

Digital Innovation Leadership:

Andhra Pradesh leads in digital governance with 85% internet penetration and 4.8 crore smartphone users. The state hosts HITEC City, one of India's largest IT clusters, and accounts for 12% of India's software development with over 1,500 IT companies.
