West Bengal: India's Cultural & Commercial Hub

West Bengal is India's fourth-largest economy by GDP, contributing over 6.2% to India's total GDP with annual revenue exceeding ₹17.8 lakh crore. Known as India's cultural capital, the state is also a major industrial center accounting for 9% of India's manufacturing output and serving as the gateway to Southeast Asia and Bangladesh.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • GDP: ₹17.84 lakh crore (2023-24)
  • Manufacturing Output: 9% of India's total
  • Port Traffic: 15% of India's cargo handling
  • Tea Production: 25% of India's tea exports
  • Jute Production: 80% of India's jute manufacturing

Digital Transformation Landscape:

West Bengal is rapidly embracing digital transformation with 76% internet penetration and 7.2 crore smartphone users. Kolkata hosts 15% of India's IT services companies and the state government's digital initiatives have created significant opportunities for business automation and digital marketing.
