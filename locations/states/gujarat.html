Gujarat: India's Business Capital

Gujarat is India's most industrialized state, contributing over 7.6% to India's GDP despite having only 5% of the population. Known for its entrepreneurial spirit, the state generates annual revenue exceeding ₹18.5 lakh crore and accounts for 22% of India's exports and 20% of industrial production.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • GDP: ₹18.55 lakh crore (2023-24)
  • Industrial Output: 20% of India's total
  • Export Contribution: 22% of India's exports
  • Chemical Production: 45% of India's chemicals
  • Port Traffic: 40% of India's cargo handling

Digital Business Environment:

Gujarat leads in digital governance with 90% government services online and 82% internet penetration. The state hosts 2.5 lakh MSMEs and 40,000+ large industries, creating massive opportunities for digital transformation and business automation.
