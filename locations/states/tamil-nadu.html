Tamil Nadu: India's Manufacturing Hub

Tamil Nadu is India's second-largest economy by GDP, contributing over 9.6% to India's total GDP and generating annual revenue exceeding ₹23.5 lakh crore. Known as the "Detroit of India," the state accounts for 35% of India's automobile production and 40% of leather exports.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • GDP: ₹23.54 lakh crore (2023-24)
  • Manufacturing Output: 35% of India's automobiles
  • IT Exports: ₹1.8 lakh crore annually
  • Textile Production: 40% of India's cotton yarn
  • Port Traffic: 30% of India's cargo handling

Digital Transformation Leadership:

Tamil Nadu leads South India in digital adoption with 86% internet penetration and 6.2 crore smartphone users. The state hosts 15% of India's IT workforce and accounts for 25% of India's software exports.
