Karnataka: India's Innovation Capital

Karnataka is India's technology powerhouse, contributing over 38% of India's IT exports and hosting 67% of India's biotechnology companies. With Bangalore as the "Silicon Valley of India," the state generates annual IT revenue exceeding ₹2.5 lakh crore and contributes 8.2% to India's GDP.

Key Economic Indicators:

  • GDP: ₹22.04 lakh crore (2023-24)
  • IT Exports: ₹2.5 lakh crore annually
  • Startup Ecosystem: 11,000+ active startups
  • R&D Investment: ₹15,000 crore annually
  • Global Capability Centers: 470+ GCCs

Digital Innovation Leadership:

Karnataka leads India in technology adoption with 88% internet penetration and 5.8 crore smartphone users. The state hosts 40% of India's unicorns and accounts for 35% of India's patent filings in technology.
