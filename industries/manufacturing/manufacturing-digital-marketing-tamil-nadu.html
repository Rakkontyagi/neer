Tamil Nadu Manufacturing Market Insights

Tamil Nadu hosts India's largest manufacturing ecosystem with over 40,000 manufacturing units and 2.5 lakh industrial workers. The state accounts for 35% of India's automobile production and generates annual manufacturing revenue exceeding ₹8.5 lakh crore.

Key Manufacturing Statistics:

  • Automotive Production: 35% of India's total
  • Manufacturing Units: 40,000+ factories
  • Industrial Workers: 2.5 lakh employed
  • Export Revenue: ₹2.8 lakh crore annually
  • Textile Production: 40% of India's cotton yarn

Digital Manufacturing Adoption:

Tamil Nadu leads in manufacturing digitization with 78% of factories adopting digital systems. 85% of manufacturers use online platforms for B2B marketing, creating massive opportunities for digital marketing and industrial automation.
