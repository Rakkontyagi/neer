Karnataka hosts India's largest concentration of educational institutions with over 200 engineering colleges, 15 universities, and 40,000+ schools. Bangalore alone accounts for 25% of India's EdTech startups and generates annual education revenue exceeding ₹45,000 crore.

  • Schools: 40,000+ primary to higher secondary
  • EdTech Startups: 25% of India's total
  • Student Population: 1.2 crore across all levels

Digital Education Adoption:
